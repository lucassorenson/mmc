  • "AMERICAN MADE" MANUFACTURING

    Multi Machining Capabilities (MMC) is a lean manufacturer of precision machine components.

We offer a full range of services to produce the parts you need when you need them. From pilot parts to prototypes to mass production, our highly skilled machinists have the expertise and capability to create unique solutions in a variety of tolerances for our customers.

Incorporated in 1998, Multi Machining Capabilities has focused on providing the highest quality CNC Machined products possible. With a diverse Equipment collection and over 100 years of combined management experience, MMC understands what is expected of them as a world class supplier.

MMC also utilizes its sister company, world class screw machine supplier Elkins Machine & Tool.
